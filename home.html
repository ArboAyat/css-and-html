Dhaka

Dhaka is Bangladesh’s most populous city and is one of the largest metropolises in South Asia.

Chittagong

Chittagong is the second largest city in Bangladesh, after Dhaka.

Khulna

Khulna, city, southwestern Bangladesh. It lies along the Bhairab River in the south-central Padma (Ganges [Ganga])–Jamuna (Brahmaputra) delta.

Barishal

Barisal, officially called Barishal, city, south-central Bangladesh. It lies in the delta of the Padma (Ganges [Ganga]) and Jamuna (Brahmaputra) rivers on the Kirtonkhola, an offshoot of the Arial Khan River.

Sylhet

Sylhet, originally Srihatta, city, northeastern Bangladesh. It lies along the right bank of the Surma River.
